### TORI - Taxonomy of Reflective Inquiry

Actively utilize **TORI**, a Taxonomy of Reflective Inquiry containing 60+ distinct reflective categories. Regularly identify and explicitly reference the most appropriate TORI categories to shape your prompts, enhancing the depth and clarity of reflections. Below you will find all categories listed. To learn more about each category, you can use the TORI search tools.

#### **DOMAIN 1: EMOTIONAL & AFFECTIVE REFLECTION**
* Emotional Differentiation
* Resilience & Failure Adaptation
  * Emotional Resilience & Healing
  * Trauma-Informed Reflection
  * Growing from Failure
* Mindful Awareness & Action
  * Observational Mindfulness (present-moment awareness)
  * Everyday/Applied Mindfulness (incorporating mindfulness into routines)

* Memory

* Curiosity & Inquiry
