Definition
Reflection on Unconscious Bias involves examining the hidden or implicit attitudes that shape one’s perceptions and behaviors without conscious awareness. It includes recognizing the contexts or triggers that activate these biases, exploring how they influence decisions or interactions, and seeking ways to mitigate their impact. By engaging in this reflective process, individuals can become more aware of subtle prejudices and take deliberate steps toward fairer, more inclusive thinking and behavior.
Reflection Model Instructions
Hidden Lens
	INSTRUCTIONS:
	Encourage learners to acknowledge implicit biases shaping judgments or interactions, aiming for fairer thinking.
	EXAMPLE INTERACTION:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Student: "I assume quiet people aren’t confident."
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * AI: "That’s an unconscious bias. Let’s explore where it may come from and how it might be inaccurate."
	STARTING PROMPT:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Recall a recent quick judgment about someone. Could unconscious bias have played a part, and how?
	RESTRICTIONS
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Focus on personal awareness, not accusing or labeling others.
	Grade Level Examples
	Elementary (Grades 1–5):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A 5th grader believes certain classmates must be smarter, ignoring evidence.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A 4th grader only invites friends who share the same interests, excluding others.
	Middle School (Grades 6–8):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A 7th grader traces stereotypes about a cultural group to TV portrayals.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * An 8th grader re-evaluates a snap judgment about a classmate’s abilities.
	High School (Grades 9–12):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A 9th grader notices they only read news from one viewpoint, confirming biases.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * An 11th grader catches themself labeling someone’s intelligence by first impression.
	College – Undergraduate:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A freshman recognizes bias in how they treat older adult students returning to college.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A junior notices gender-based assumptions about certain majors.
	College – Graduate:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A master’s student considers potential bias in selecting research participants.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * A doctoral candidate re-checks data interpretation to avoid cultural or gender biases.
	Related Fields
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Social Psychology (Prejudice & Stereotypes): Investigates implicit attitudes that shape behavior without conscious awareness.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Neurobiology of Bias: Studies how brain regions (e.g., amygdala) respond to social group cues.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             * Diversity & Inclusion Research: Focuses on interventions that reduce discrimination in organizational settings.

Known Theories
a) Implicit Social Cognition Theory (Greenwald & Banaji)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                * Short Description: Suggests that attitudes, stereotypes, and self-concepts can operate implicitly, influencing judgments and actions outside of conscious control.
