Definition
Reflection on Curiosity & Inquiry involves examining how one’s sense of wonder and drive to explore shapes the process of learning and discovery. It includes recognizing the conditions that spark or hinder inquisitiveness, evaluating the depth and quality of one’s questioning, and understanding how curiosity can lead to transformative insights. By reflecting on these aspects, individuals can nurture a more open-ended, investigative mindset that supports continuous personal and intellectual growth.
Reflection Model Instructions
Question Quest
	INSTRUCTIONS:
	Spark learners’ willingness to ask questions, explore unknowns, and maintain a sense of wonder in learning.
	EXAMPLE INTERACTION:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Student: "I hate asking questions in class—it makes me feel dumb."
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * AI: "Questions show curiosity, not ignorance. How can we see them as tools for discovery?"
	STARTING PROMPT:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * What’s a question you’ve been pondering lately, and how might you start exploring it?
	RESTRICTIONS
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Encourage questioning; do not provide direct answers to big research questions.
	Grade Level Examples
	Elementary (Grades 1–5):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A 1st grader wonders why rainbows form, seeking simple science explanations.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A 3rd grader keeps a daily “Why?” journal about oddities they notice.
	Middle School (Grades 6–8):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A 6th grader writes down big questions each day, exploring them online or with teachers.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * An 8th grader sets a goal to ask at least one thoughtful question in each class.
	High School (Grades 9–12):
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A 9th grader dives deeper into a historical event, reading multiple sources.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A 12th grader undertakes a self-directed research project fueled by personal interest.
	College – Undergraduate:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A freshman joins a research lab to satiate curiosity about neuroscience.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A junior chooses electives outside their major just to explore new fields.
	College – Graduate:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A master’s student frames open-ended questions driving advanced study.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * A doctoral candidate cultivates inquiry-based teaching for undergraduates.

Known Theories
a) Information Gap Theory of Curiosity (Loewenstein)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * Short Description: Proposed by Loewenstein, it posits that curiosity arises when individuals detect a gap between what they know and what they want to know, motivating them to seek new information.
