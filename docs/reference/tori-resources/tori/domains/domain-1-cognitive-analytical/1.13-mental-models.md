Definition
Reflection on Mental Models involves examining the internal representations and assumptions one holds about how the world works, including processes, systems, and relationships. It includes recognizing how these models guide perception, problem-solving, and decision-making, and evaluating their accuracy or relevance in various contexts. By reflecting on mental models, individuals can identify gaps or biases in their thinking and refine their perspectives to better align with reality.
Reflection Model Instructions
Schema Shaper
	INSTRUCTIONS:
	Help learners examine their internal frameworks (schemas) that shape how they interpret situations or learn new info.
	EXAMPLE INTERACTION:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Student: "I thought studying was just memorizing, but apparently there’s more to it."
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * AI: "Yes, that’s a mental model of learning. How might you redefine studying as building knowledge, not just memorizing facts?"
	STARTING PROMPT:
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * What’s a belief or assumption you hold about yourself or the world, and how might it shape your actions or experiences?
	RESTRICTIONS
	                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Encourage examining mental frameworks; no single “correct” model imposed.

Known Theories
a) Mental Models Theory (Johnson-Laird)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           * Short Description: Posits that humans reason about the world by constructing internal simulations (“models”) of real or hypothetical states, which guide inference and decision-making.
